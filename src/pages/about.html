About

This site allows searching for flights on many dates to many destinations simultaniously. This is often the search I want to do for myself but I can't find a site to do it this way.

Sometimes, I know the destination, and more or less the dates. In this situation, I am happy with the well known flight tickets sites. Very often, I just want to go somewhere when I will have time, and I am open for sugestions from Oslo to Barcelona. I base my choice on flight times, destination and price. I aim to make this search possible in a single page, just filtering the availbale flights and never having to ask for data to reload to match my changed search criteria. And I could leave Friday evening or Saturday morning, or a week later if necessary, and want that as one search criteria, not as two separate searches.

Q&A

Q: Where does the flight data come from

A: At the moment, the site shows only transavia flights, with data provided by Transavia. Flight information is updated once daily, so there is a small chance that a flight you see here is not available to book. The site will be extended to show data about other flights provided by Google QPX Express API
